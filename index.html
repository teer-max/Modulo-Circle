Overview


I believe this is a pretty structure in mathematics. What is seen in most of these apps is a visualisation of a special multiplication table. What's happening is that we are looking at a bunch of numbers, or inputs, arranged around a circle from zero up to some limit. We see colorful lines connecting each number to another which are a visualisation of the mappings of all of the inputs on the circle to their corresponding outputs on the circle. The outputs are determined by the limit, or the modulus, and the multiplier. As you play the animation in the first link, the multiplier is continuously increased. This starts to create some interesting patterns, and you are welcome to think of interesting cases, like when the number in the multiplier divides the number in the modulus. You can edit these values via the sliders to see how it effects the animation and test out values for yourself.
